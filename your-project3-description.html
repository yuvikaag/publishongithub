EK KAHANI

The is a retelling or narrating a known story in a style that I was inspired by. The project formats was a composition / compilation/ artwork made on an A1 sheet with exactly 15 frames that are used to visually narrate the story. I looked at languages and contexts that are varied in nature and context and explored various styles and skills learned throughout.

Story

Everybody in town was busy in the preparation for Christmas. There was a famous toy shop in that town. Variety of toys could be found there. Among all the toys, there was a pretty and beautiful doll named, 'Suzie'. She stood in a corner as her arm was missing so nobody wanted to buy her. Children came to the toy shop with their parents and bought video games , dolls etc. but nobody wanted to but Suzie. One day a beautiful and disabled girl came to the toy shop with her mother. The girl replied,''she is a very beautiful doll. Even if her arm is broken, I will take her. We will stitch her arms. She looks lonely in that corner. She too needs love like the other toys''. After listening her good thought Mr. Brown immediately stitched the arms and gave her a new look. Now, Suzie became the prettiest doll. Mr. Brown gave the doll to Suzie. The girl, her mother and Suzie went back home. The girl did not know that Suzie would come as a blessing in her life. The girl would play with Suzie everyday and she used to love her a lot. After a month, she became fine and was able to walk properly. Suzie came as an angel in disguise in her life. MORAL - The only disability in life is a negative attitude.
